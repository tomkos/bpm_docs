# Selecting favorite objects in the Business Integration perspective

If you work frequently with certain artifacts in the Business
Integration perspective, and want to make them easy to find, you can
mark them as favorites. If you have marked an artifact as a favorite,
you can remove it from your favorites, if necessary.

## About this task

## Procedure

1. Right-click an artifact in the Business Integration view.
2. Select Add To Favorites from the
menu.  The artifact will display in boldface type
at the top of the folder in which it resides. Favorites are excluded
from filtering.
3. To remove an artifact as a favorite, right-click it and
then click Remove From Favorites.
