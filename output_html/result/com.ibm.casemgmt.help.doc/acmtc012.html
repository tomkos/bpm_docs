Cannot reassign work items in the All Assigned Work in-basket

Symptoms

Causes

Your role does not have the permission to reassign work.

Resolving the problem

  • In Case Client, go to Manage Roles, and add you to a role that has permission to reassign work.
  • In Case Builder, go to the Roles tab, and locate your role. For the Work assignment options to display for this role section, select the Role members can reassign work to others option.
