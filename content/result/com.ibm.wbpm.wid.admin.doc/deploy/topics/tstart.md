## About this task

## Procedure

1. In the Business Integration or Debug perspective, click
the Servers tab to open the Servers view.
2 In the Servers view, right-click your server and selectone of the following server modes from the menu: These two modes are the most common means of starting a server in IBM IntegrationDesigner .Note: These options are disabled for remote Workflow Center Servers.
    - Debug
    - Start
3 In the Servers view, ensure that the Status areadisplays one of the following status messages before you continuewith your development activities:

- Debugging
- Started
