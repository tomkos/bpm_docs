# Setting up collaboration features for business users

## About this task

- Enabling Sametime Connect integration for Process Portal (deprecated)

You can enable instant messaging support to allow your end users to communicate in real time with others in the organization as they work on process activities.
- Specifying experts for an activity

Business users working with your process applications can collaborate or request assistance from expert users associated with a given task or activity. The list of experts appears in the Experts panel in the Process Portal environment.
- Enabling IBM Connections integration for Process Portal (deprecated)

If your environment is configured for IBM Connections V4.0 or later, you can set up Process Portal so that users can, after they set preferences, receive notifications in IBM Connections when new tasks are assigned to them.You can also enable the display of business card information when users click an avatar or a name.
