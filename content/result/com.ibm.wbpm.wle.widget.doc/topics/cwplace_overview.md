# Managing work

- Completing work in Workplace

From the start page, you can organize and work on your tasks, and also use workflows or cases to  complete structured or unstructured work.
- Managing work and performance for your teams

As a team manager, you can also manage the work of the teams you're responsible for in Workplace. You can use the data in the Teams dashboard to evaluate your teams' workload and act on their performance.
