# Modifying the properties of an activity

## About this task

If the Properties view
is not shown in your perspective, you can right-click the activity
and choose Show in Properties.

## Procedure

1. On the canvas, click the activity that you want to modify.
2. In the properties area, click the tab with the properties
you want to modify and make the appropriate changes.
