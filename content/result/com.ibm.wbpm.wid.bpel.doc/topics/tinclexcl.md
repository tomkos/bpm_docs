# Including and excluding calendars

## About this task

## Procedure

1. From the business calendar editor, include a calendar by
clicking Add Calendar to the Time Intervals
list or exclude a calendar by clicking Add Calendar to
the Exceptions list.
2. From the Business Calendar window, select a business calendar
from the list to include or exclude. Note that you can create a new
business calendar from this window if required by clicking New.
3. Click OK. The business calendar
is added to appropriate list in the editor depending on whether you
are including or excluding.
