Verifying solution deployment

Procedure

To verify the solution:

  1. Log in to Case Client.
  2. From the list of available solutions, expand your solution, and click Manage Roles.
  3. Click Add Case to add a case to the solution. Verify that the preconditions for the case are met.
  4. Open and complete a work item from the in-basket to verify that the page works correctly.
  5. Verify that case types, tasks, and roles are created and working. To verify that the other components in the solution work correctly, open and test other pages and cases. You can search for cases by going to the case pages and searching for a case by date.
