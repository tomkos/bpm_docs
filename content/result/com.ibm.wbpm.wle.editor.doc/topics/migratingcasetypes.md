# Migrating case types

1. Open the process app that contains your case types. They appear under
Processes in the library.
2. Click the case type. You are prompted to open the case type.
3. Click View to open the case type in read-only mode.
4. Click Open to convert the case type into a process. Note: You
cannot revert to a case type after it is converted to a process.

- Converting case types from IBM Business Process Manager V8.5.5

After you convert a case type that you created in Business Automation Workflow V8.5.5 to a process, you can add functions that were added in V 8.5.6, such as data change management for case details UIs.
