# Integration Designer samples

## Samples included with the product

Click the links in the table of contents on the left to see the documentation for the business
integration samples that are included with the product. You can access these samples and their
instructions from Help > Samples and Tutorials > IBM Integration
Designer.

## Install your own samples into the product

You can use IBM Integration
Designer to make your own
samples. You can create custom samples for your customers or coworkers, and put them on a local or
remote site. The user can then install and view the samples from Help > Samples and Tutorials > IBM Integration
Designer.
