# Opening the Business Integration perspective

By default, the Business Integration perspective opens
when you launch the product. You can switch between opened perspectives
by selecting them at the upper-right corner of the Workbench. In the
title of the Workbench window, you can see the name of the active
perspective.

## About this task

To open the Business
Integration perspective, complete the following steps:

## Procedure

1. Click the Open Perspective button  on the upper right corner of the Workbench window. This provides
the same function as clicking Window > Open Perspective on the menu
bar.
2. To see a complete list of perspectives, right-click and
then click Other
3. Select the Business Integration perspective. If you do
not see the perspective, check the Show all check box.
