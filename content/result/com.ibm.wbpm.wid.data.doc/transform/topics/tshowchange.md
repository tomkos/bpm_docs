# Creating maps using the show change and event summaries

## About this task

## Procedure

1. Click the Show change summary and event summary
on business objects button on the toolbar:
2. You can then create maps between the change and event summaries
in the source and target business objects.Note: If mapping from a business graph
to a business object, the business graph information will be lost.
This includes change summary, event summary, and verb. Also, transforms
involving the change summary and event summary of a business object
may not be recognized. They will only be recognized when the map is
invoked as a submap within the context of a business graph.
