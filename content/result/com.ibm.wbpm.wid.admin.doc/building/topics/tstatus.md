# Viewing the status of projects

## Before you begin

## About this task

## Procedure

1. In the Business Integration perspective, click the Build
Activities tab. (If the Build Activities tab
is not visible, open either the Business Integration view or the Physical
Resources view and click the Show Build Activities View icon.)
The Build Activities view opens.
2. In the Build Activities view, scroll down and expand the Project
status section. The Project status section is displayed.
