# Validating pages in client-side human services

To ensure that a page that is in your client-side human service passes valid data in the
flow, use the page validation pattern to check its data.

In addition to the following topics, see the topic Validating data in client-side human services.

- Validating data without exiting the coach

You can use the data change properties of a coach in a client-side human service to validate the coach data without exiting the coach.
- Validating coach data after exiting a coach

To validate the coach data in a client-side human service after exiting the coach, you can use a called service or a client-side script to validate the coach data.
