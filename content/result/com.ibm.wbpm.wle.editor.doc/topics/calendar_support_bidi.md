# Heritage coaches: Configuring a Hebrew or Islamic calendar (deprecated)

## About this task

## Procedure

1. Open the desktop Process Designer.
2. Open a Heritage Human service.
3. In the Diagram page, add or select
a heritage coach.  To add a heritage coach, scroll to the
bottom of the palette.
4. Open the heritage coach.
5. From the Controls section of the
palette, drag a Date Selection control onto
the editor.
6. In the Properties view, switch to the Presentation tab.
7. Select Hebrew or Islamic in
the National Calendars list.
8. Run the Coach. The Hebrew or Islamic calendar
is displayed in the date selector, instead of the Gregorian calendar.
9. You can also change a preference in Process Portal to
display the Hebrew or Islamic calendar.
For more information, see Setting preferences in Heritage Process Portal
